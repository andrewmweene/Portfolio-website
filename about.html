About Me

Hello! I'm Andrew Buumba Mweene — a passionate Software Engineering student from Zambia building the future, one line of code at a time.

My Journey

I grew up in Chibombo, Zambia, where curiosity about how things work led me to take apart radios and old computers as a kid. That curiosity turned into passion when I discovered programming — the ability to create something real from pure logic still amazes me every day.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Science at The University of Zambia, I’m specializing in Software Engineering because I believe great software can solve real problems — from improving education access to helping local businesses grow.

When I'm not coding, you’ll find me reading tech blogs, playing football with friends, or teaching basic computer skills to kids in my community.

Quick Facts
